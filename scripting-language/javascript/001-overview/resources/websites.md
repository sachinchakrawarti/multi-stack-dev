# 📖 Introduction

The following websites are highly recommended for learning JavaScript, practicing coding, preparing for interviews, and staying updated with the latest ECMAScript features.

These resources are organized by category to help you choose the right platform based on your learning goals.

- Frequently updated

- Interview preparation
