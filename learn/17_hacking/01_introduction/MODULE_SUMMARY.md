# Important Concepts to Remember

Always remember these key principles:

- Ethics come before technical skills.
- Never test systems without authorization.
- Follow the Rules of Engagement.
- Protect confidential information.
- Practice responsible vulnerability disclosure.
- Learn continuously.
- Document your work.
- Stay curious and keep improving.
