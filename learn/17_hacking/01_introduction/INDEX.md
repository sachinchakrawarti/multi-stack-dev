# Module 01 – Introduction to Ethical Hacking

Welcome to **Module 01 – Introduction to Ethical Hacking**.

This module builds the foundation required to understand cybersecurity and ethical hacking. Before learning networking, web security, penetration testing, or exploit development, it is important to understand the core concepts, terminology, legal responsibilities, and career paths in cybersecurity.

By the end of this module, you will have a solid understanding of the ethical hacking ecosystem and be ready to continue with **Networking Fundamentals**.

# Prerequisites

No prior cybersecurity knowledge is required.

Basic computer skills are recommended, including:

- Using Windows or Linux
- Browsing the web
- Basic command-line usage
- Interest in technology and cybersecurity

---

# Learning Tips

- 📖 Read every lesson carefully.
- 💻 Practice concepts in safe and authorized environments.
- 📝 Take notes while learning.
- 🔍 Research unfamiliar terms.
- 🤝 Follow ethical and legal practices at all times.
- 🚀 Complete the practice exercises after each lesson.
