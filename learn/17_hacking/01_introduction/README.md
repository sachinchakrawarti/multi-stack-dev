# 🛡️ Module 01 – Introduction to Ethical Hacking

> **"Every cybersecurity expert was once a beginner."**

Welcome to **Module 01 – Introduction to Ethical Hacking**.

This module is the starting point of your cybersecurity journey. It introduces the core concepts, terminology, ethics, legal responsibilities, career paths, and learning roadmap that every aspiring ethical hacker should understand before moving to practical topics.

Whether your goal is to become an **Ethical Hacker**, **Penetration Tester**, **Security Engineer**, **SOC Analyst**, or **Bug Bounty Hunter**, this module provides the essential foundation for everything that follows.

---

# 🎯 Module Objectives

After completing this module, you will be able to:

- Understand what hacking is and its purpose.
- Differentiate between ethical and malicious hacking.
- Identify different types of hackers.
- Understand the ethical hacking methodology.
- Learn the Cyber Kill Chain.
- Explore cybersecurity domains.
- Understand Red Team vs Blue Team operations.
- Learn legal and ethical responsibilities.
- Understand vulnerability disclosure.
- Become familiar with common cybersecurity terminology.
- Explore cybersecurity career opportunities.
- Build a structured cybersecurity learning roadmap.

# 💻 Prerequisites

No prior cybersecurity experience is required.

Helpful skills include:

- Basic computer knowledge
- Windows or Linux usage
- Basic internet browsing
- Curiosity and willingness to learn

---

# 📌 Best Practices While Learning

- 📖 Read every lesson completely.
- 💻 Practice only in legal and authorized environments.
- 📝 Take notes.
- 🔍 Research unfamiliar terms.
- 🧪 Complete the practice exercises.
- 📚 Review previous lessons regularly.
- ⚖️ Always follow ethical and legal guidelines.
