## Overview

**System Design** is the process of defining the architecture, components, modules, interfaces, and data flow of a system to satisfy specific functional and non-functional requirements. It bridges the gap between business problems and technical solutions.

<div className="info-box">
  <strong>💡 Key Insight:</strong> Good system design is about making the right trade-offs between competing concerns like performance, cost, and complexity.
</div>
